The Internet is a Black Box

When you visit a website, you share your IP, location, and device data. But where does your data go? Who is hosting it? How many third-party trackers are watching your every move? We believe infrastructure transparency should be a fundamental right.

Hidden Trackers

Websites inject invisible pixels and scripts to track your browsing habits across the web.

Server Uncertainty

Data sovereignty matters. Knowing which country stores your data affects your legal rights.

Privacy Concerns

Without inspecting the TLS/SSL certs and WHOIS data, establishing trust is nearly impossible.

Everything you need to know

NetPin provides a comprehensive breakdown of the digital infrastructure.

Location Detection

Map exactly where the server is located globally, including city, region, and distance from you.

Hosting & ASN

Identify the hosting provider, ISP, and Autonomous System Number (ASN) routing your connection.

Security Insights

Analyze TLS/SSL inspection, privacy jurisdictions, and compute a comprehensive connection risk score.

Tracker Detection

Uncover hidden cookies, tracking pixels, and third-party services loaded on the current page.

Carbon Awareness

Verify if the server runs on renewable energy and view its carbon efficiency and green certification.

Export Reports

Generate and download comprehensive JSON, CSV, or TXT reports of the analyzed infrastructure.

How NetPin Works

A seamless process from installation to deep insights.

Step 1

Install Extension

Download and load the NetPin extension into your Chromium browser in developer mode.

Step 2

Visit Any Website

Browse the internet normally. NetPin works silently in the background analyzing the active tab.

Step 3

Analyze Infrastructure

NetPin fetches server location, runs WHOIS lookups, detects trackers, and checks carbon records.

Step 4

View Insights & Reports

Open the interactive NetPin dashboard to view real-time data, security scores, and export detailed reports.

Your data stays yours.

NetPin is designed with a strict privacy-first architecture. We never collect your browsing history or sell your data.

Local Processing

Data analysis occurs directly on your machine or via our secure intermediary APIs without logging your origin IP. We do not maintain a database of your queries.

Open Source

NetPin is built transparently. Our codebase is open source, allowing you and the community to audit the code and ensure there are no hidden telemetry scripts.

How to Install

Since NetPin is not yet published on the Web Store, please follow these simple manual installation steps:

1

Download

Download the NetPin ZIP file and extract it.

2

Open Extensions

Navigate to chrome://extensions in your browser.

3

Developer Mode

Toggle "Developer mode" on in the top right corner.

4

Load Unpacked

Click "Load unpacked" and select the extracted folder.
